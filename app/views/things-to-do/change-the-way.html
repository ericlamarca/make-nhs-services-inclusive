Consider co-designing your content

Involve your users or charities that represent them. You may be able to draw on internal staff networks.

When we co-design, we share power more equally. Share power at every stage of your project: in research, decision-making, design, delivery and evaluation. Make it an ongoing conversation.

Example

We designed a new video player, so that it could show British Sign Language (BSL) versions of videos. We worked with our internal Deaf and hard of hearing group and with charities and iterated our prototypes with them..
